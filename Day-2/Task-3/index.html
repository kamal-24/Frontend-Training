Nikola Tesla's contibution towards science:

  • 1889 - In 1889 Tesla invented Tesla coil which proved the existence of electomagentic raditaion.

  • 1890 - He attempted to develop a wireless lighting based on near-field inductive conducted a series of public demonstrations.

  • 1892 to 1894- Served as vice-president of America Institute of Electrical Engineers curently(IEEE).

  • 1895 - In 1895 Edward Dean Adams collabrated with Sir Tesla and started Nikola Tesla Company.

  • 1896 - X-ray experimentation : Tesla said he could feel a sharp stinging pain where it entered his body, and again at the place where it passed out. In comparing these particles with projected by his "electric gun," Tesla said, "The particles in the beam of force ... will travel much faster than such particles ... and they will travel in concentrations".

  • 1898 - Radio-Control : In 1898, Tesla demonstrated a boat that used a coherer-based radio control—which he dubbed "telautomaton"—to the public during an electrical exhibition at Madison Square Garden.

  • 1898-? - Wireless power : From the 1890s through 1906, Tesla spent a great deal of his time on a series of projects trying to develop the transmission of electricity without wires. It was an expansion of his idea of using coils to transmit power that he had been demonstrating in wireless lighting.To create a way to transmit worldwide communications.

  • Eidetic memory - Tesla read many works, memorizing complete books, and supposedly possessed a photographic memory.He was a polyglot, speaking eight languages: Serbo-Croatian, Czech, English, French, German, Hungarian, Italian, and Latin.
